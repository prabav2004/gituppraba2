Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 6-7 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Chop tomatoes and mix with garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slice baguette and toast in the oven for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Top toasted baguette slices with tomato mixture and serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 150, Fats: 5g, Proteins: 3g, Carbs: 22g
